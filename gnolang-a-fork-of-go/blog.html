...But the thing is since GNO is a baby block chain right now, we don't know the answers to this, and part of my interest comes from not knowing the answers.
The quote above, from Jacob Gadikian's blog, is not the only reason why I'm interested in the GNO project. It is also why I'm interested in blockchain technology as a whole. Blockchain is very young when you zoom out. The infinite questions that we have yet to find answers to have fueled my curiosity into a fantastic ball of excitement. This curiosity is largely why I started my Journey to Blockchain Development. It is essentially why I'm taking this nanodgree program and creating this fake blog.
